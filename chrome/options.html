General settings











Alarm and notifications

The audible alarm can draw your attention when certain events occur. It can also compel others to appease your computer with a hammer, if you leave it logged into Pardus while you are not around to snooze it.

Alarm sound:







Desktop notifications are subtler and convenient when you are at your computer, just not watching the Pardus tab.







Combat settings






Robot autofill fills the "use robots" field with the amount needed to repair your ship's armour. For this, you need to configure your ship's armour points and strength.

Ambush settings

Alliance quicklists are obtained from the in-game My Alliance page; see here for details.







Online list highlighting

Highlight specific pilots in the online list. Write one name per line, or separate names with commas.




Clocks

Countdowns to recurring in-game events. For these to be accurate, your computer's clock and time zone should be set correctly. Modern operating systems can keep your clock on the correct time automatically; see this article for a quick rundown.
